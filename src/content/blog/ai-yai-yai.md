From the moment I first installed and typed "Claude" at my raspi command prompt I have been blown away by what is possible with this tool.  The Star Trek character Captain Jean Luke Picard had an Android, Commander Data, next to him providing him with the sum total of the universe's knowledge at his finger tips whenever it was needed.  That is how I feel when I wake up my instance of Claude and begin solving, building, learning and above all else saving a tonne of time to get things done.

Data does not replace the good Captain and A.I. does not have to replace us -  when properly managed - A.I. tools augment our skills so we get more done faster and move forward more effectively in our journeys. 

My setup is simple.  I have Claude running on one of my rasperry pi (5) boards and it runs very well in that space. I have build 3 projects with Claude's "help" and I have many more thoughts about how I can benefit from the vast coding skills that I can tap into with Claude Code.
