Hello! I'm Igor and I've had the privilege to work in IT for the better part of 40 years. Working as a senior leader in a large IT vendor put me on the front lines of the evolution of compute, storage and networking technologies. 

While my main hobby interests revolved around wood and glass,  I always had my foot in the door of automation. We're talking as far back as the X10 days. Watching that world change was itself a hobby but it really grew when a friend pointed me to Home Assistant - that's when things got very interesting. 

When I discovered the fun in Arduino, Tasmoto and ultimately settled on ESPhome I became way more of an electronics hobbyist than I ever imagined. If your still reading this you know what I mean when I say I lit things up, then I controlled something, then I dimmed something then I displayed something etc. Each learning was a revelation and fed the need for the next project.   

My purpose with this site is just to share what I can as I make projects - there may be someone thinking the same, doing similar things and seeing how others do the task. That is the whole fun and every investigation is a learning opportunity.
